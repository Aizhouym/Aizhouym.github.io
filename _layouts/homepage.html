Research Interests

My work sits at the intersection of large language models and software engineering / security. Specifically, I focus on three directions: (1) LLM Agent design — how a single agent can plan, reason, and act in open-ended software tasks; (2) Multi-Agent Systems — how multiple specialized agents can be orchestrated to collaboratively generate structured outputs such as process models and code; and (3) Neural-Symbolic Methods — combining formal symbolic representations with neural reasoning to improve the reliability and verifiability of agent behavior.
