## About Me

Hi! I am a first-year CS PhD student at the **[Text Mining Lab](https://liususan091219.github.io/)**, **Stevens Institute of Technology**, where I am fortunate to be advised by **[Prof. Xueqing Liu](https://liususan091219.github.io/)**.
